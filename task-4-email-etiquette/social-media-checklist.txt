Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s:

1. Use respectful and polite language online
2. Think before posting any content
3. Protect your personal information
4. Verify information before sharing
5. Use social media for learning and networking

Don’ts:

1. Do not share fake news or misinformation
2. Do not post offensive or harmful content
3. Do not share personal details publicly
4. Do not engage in cyberbullying
5. Do not waste excessive time on social media
