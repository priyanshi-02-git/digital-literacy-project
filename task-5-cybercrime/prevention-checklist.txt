Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

You must protect your UPI PIN and OTP from all people. 
2. You should not scan any QR codes which you cannot identify. 
3. You should verify all payment requests before you give your approval. 
4. Your passwords should contain strong elements and need to be different from each other. 
5. You should avoid clicking on links and messages which appear suspicious. 
6. You should not enter your personal details on websites which you do not recognize. 
7. You should enable two-factor authentication for all your online accounts. 
8. You need to perform bank statement checks on a regular basis.

UPI Safety Tips:
9. Remember: you do NOT need PIN to receive money
10. Only send money to trusted contacts
